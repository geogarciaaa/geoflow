👋 about GeoFlow

Built by a student. For real businesses.

Hey — I'm Geo. I'm a Business & Engineering student at Drexel University in Philadelphia, and GeoFlow started because of my dad.

Growing up, I worked with him in his landscaping business. He'd be out on jobs all day — hands dirty, crew to manage, no time to answer every call. A lot of those calls went to voicemail. A lot of those jobs went to someone else.

He wasn't bad at his work — he was great. He just had no system to catch the leads he missed. That stuck with me. When I got to Drexel and learned to build software, I knew exactly what to solve first.

the problem

40-60% of leads go to voicemail and never call back

It's happening to local businesses everywhere, every single day. They work hard, they're good at what they do — but they lose jobs before they even get to quote them. It's not a skill problem. It's a follow-up problem.

the idea

What if the follow-up happened automatically — in 2 minutes?

Text someone back within 2 minutes of a missed call and there's a good chance they respond. Wait an hour and they've hired someone else. So I built exactly that: the moment a call is missed, the caller gets a personal text back. No app, no workflow change. It just runs.

why me

Not a tech genius — just someone who saw a real problem

I'm a freshman studying Business & Engineering. I won't pretend I've been coding for 10 years. But I saw a real problem, learned what I needed, and built something that works. GeoFlow is me — figuring it out, staying accountable, and delivering for the people I work with.

the mission

Help local businesses compete with the big guys

Big franchises have call centers and CRMs. Local businesses have one guy and a cell phone. GeoFlow levels the field — the same automated follow-up power as a company 10× your size, at a price that makes sense.
