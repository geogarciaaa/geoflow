👋 About GeoFlow

Built by a student.
For real businesses.

Hey — I'm Geo. I'm a Business & Engineering student at Drexel University in Philadelphia, and GeoFlow started because of my dad.

Growing up, I worked with him in his landscaping business. He'd be out on jobs all day — hands dirty, crew to manage, no time to stop and answer every call. A lot of those calls went to voicemail. A lot of those voicemails never got called back. And a lot of those jobs went to someone else.

It wasn't because he wasn't good at what he did — he was great. He just didn't have a system to catch the leads he missed. That stuck with me. When I got to Drexel and started learning how to actually build software, I knew exactly what I wanted to solve first.

The Problem

40-60% of leads go to voicemail and never call back

My dad was one of those guys. Out on jobs all day, doing great work — but missing calls and losing jobs to competitors who just happened to pick up. He wasn't alone. It's happening to local businesses everywhere, every single day. They work hard, they're good at what they do, but they lose jobs before they even get a chance to quote them. It's not a skill problem. It's a follow-up problem.

The Idea

What if the follow-up happened automatically — in 2 minutes?

I started thinking about this when I noticed how fast people move on. If you text someone back within 2 minutes of a missed call, there's still a good chance they respond. Wait an hour and they've already hired someone else. So I built a system that does exactly that — the moment a call is missed, the person gets a personal text back instantly. No app, no change to how the business operates. It just runs in the background.

Why Me

I'm a freshman at Drexel — not a tech genius, just someone who saw a real problem

I'm a freshman at Drexel University studying Business & Engineering. I'm not going to pretend I've been coding for 10 years — I haven't. But I saw a real problem, learned what I needed to learn, and built something that works. GeoFlow isn't some big agency with a team behind it. It's me, figuring it out, staying accountable, and making sure it actually delivers for the people I work with. That's the whole thing.

The Mission

Help local businesses compete with the big guys

Big franchises have entire call centers and CRM systems. Local businesses have one guy and a cell phone. GeoFlow levels the playing field — giving small businesses the same automated follow-up power as a company 10x their size, at a price that actually makes sense.
