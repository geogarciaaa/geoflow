⚙️ how it works

Four steps. Zero new software.

GeoFlow runs quietly in the background. No app to babysit, no workflow to relearn — just missed calls turning into booked jobs while your crew stays on the tools.

1

Call gets missed

GeoFlow spots the unanswered inbound call in real time.

2

Sarah texts back

A warm, human message goes out in ~3 seconds — asks what's wrong and where.

3

Reply captured

Issue, zip, and urgency are pulled from the reply and sorted for you.

4

Lands on your phone

Hottest lead first, in one clean queue. Tap to call. Booked.

🤝 human handoff

When it needs a human, it gets one.

A pricing question? A specific 1994 Trane model? GeoFlow never fakes an answer — it flags the lead and loops you in.

They ask10:13 AM

"Do you take Amex? And can you fix a 1994 Trane XL1200?"

GeoFlow10:13 AM

"Great questions — those are ones for Mike directly. I'm flagging this as a priority so he can text you right back. You're at 78701, correct?"

🔔 Flagged for a human — sent to your phone now.

  1. It reads the message

    Spots pricing, technical, or off-script questions it shouldn't answer alone.

  2. It never guesses

    No made-up quotes or specs. It keeps the tone warm and buys you a minute.

  3. It hands off

    Flags the lead urgent and pings you to jump in — with the full conversation attached.
